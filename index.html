No matter if you are looking for garage tile, floor epoxy, or garage mats, GarageFloor.com is the only place you need to turn to for all of your flooring needs!

Our selection of top-quality garage tile is a great option for covering and protecting older, damaged and stained floors, as well as for keeping new floors looking new. Our tiles are available in a wide range of patterns and colors, so you can create virtually any look for your space! Tiles can be installed in no time with no previous experience necessary, little or no surface preparation, messy adhesives, or hours and hours of your time. Interlocking tile is one of the easiest ways to protect the floors of your garage, workshop, or basement!

Floor mats and containment mats are another great option for your flooring needs. Mats for your garage are available in various patterns and incredibly easy to install and can be used instantly. Mats provide a vapor barrier, hide blemishes and cushion your steps. Containment mats keep your floor clean where you need it the most under your vehicle. Containment mats are mobile and super easy to clean. More importantly, containment mats keep snow and water off your garage floor, making it safer to walk.

Garage coatings are also a great option. We carry Rust Bullet urethane-based coatings as an alternative to epoxy. Simply put, in 13 years in the industry, we have never found a product as effective as Polyurea is on the garage, shop, industrial or commercial surface. Unlike epoxy or paint, Polyurea will not lift from hot tires.
